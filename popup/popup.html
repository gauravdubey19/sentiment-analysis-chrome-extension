Select Text → Click FAB → View Results
1
Select Text

Highlight any text on the page — a sentence, paragraph, or any block of content.

2
Click "Analyze"

A floating purple button appears near your selection. Click it — or right-click and choose Analyze Sentiment from the context menu.

3
Review Results

The page blurs, revealing a panel with the sentiment verdict (Positive / Negative / Neutral), a confidence score, and a word-level breakdown.

4
Adjust & Re-Analyze

Use the range sliders to trim the start/end of your selection, then click Re-Analyze for a refined result.

5
Highlight & Export

Click Highlight on Page to mark the analyzed text. Export your history as JSON or CSV from this popup.
